## What Are Rife Frequencies?

Rife frequency therapy originates from the work of **Dr. Royal Raymond Rife** in the early 20th century. Rife proposed that every microorganism—bacteria, viruses, fungi, parasites—has a unique natural frequency, much like a musical instrument has a specific pitch. By exposing a pathogen to its exact resonant frequency, he believed it was possible to cause the microorganism to vibrate intensely enough to rupture its structure, thereby neutralizing it without damaging surrounding healthy tissue.

Although Rife's methods were controversial and never adopted by mainstream medicine, the concept of using targeted frequencies for wellness has persisted in alternative and complementary health circles. Modern Rife practice often involves listening to pure tones or complex waveforms, with the idea that these sound vibrations can interact with the body's cells and energy fields to promote balance, support immune function, and address specific health concerns.

*   49.2 Hz × 2 = 98.4 Hz (3rd octave)
